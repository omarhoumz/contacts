# Pricing and Monetization

## Model

Open-core product with paid hosted convenience.

## Pricing Direction

- Free: limited usage for acquisition
- Lifetime Lite: `$19-29` one-time
- Personal Yearly: `$12-18/year`
- Family Yearly: `$24-36/year`

## Margin Target

- Maintain `20-35%` gross margin above infra + support cost.

## MAU and Cost Bands (Rough)


| Phase   | MAU target       | Estimated monthly cost |
| ------- | ---------------- | ---------------------- |
| Phase 1 | `500-3,000`      | `~$64-120`             |
| Phase 2 | `3,000-15,000`   | `~$90-220`             |
| Phase 3 | `15,000-60,000`  | `~$180-500`            |
| Phase 4 | `60,000-120,000` | `~$350-1,000+`         |


## Pricing Guardrails

- Keep one-time plan feature-capped to protect margins.
- Re-evaluate pricing every 2-3 months using real support and infra data.
